Requirements for Military Service

Citizenship

Must be an Egyptian citizen with valid national identification.

Age

Applicants must be between 18 and 35 years of age.

Health

Must be in good physical and mental health as verified by medical examination.

Education

Minimum high school diploma or equivalent qualification required.

Background

Must have a clean criminal record and pass security clearance.

Commitment

Ready to commit to military service and uphold the nation's values.

General Notes

Processing Time

Applications are typically reviewed within 30 days. You will receive updates on your email and phone number provided during registration.

Confidentiality

All personal information provided is confidential and will only be used for recruitment purposes in accordance with Egyptian military regulations.

Equal Opportunity

The Egyptian Military is an equal opportunity employer. We evaluate all applications fairly and without discrimination.
